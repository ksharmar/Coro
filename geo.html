Coronavirus on Social Media: Analyzing Geographical Distribution


Social media collects posts from users around the world. After analyzing over 2 million tweets with geographical information collected from 2019-03-09 to 2020-03-17, we show the daily geographical distribution of the count of tweets (in log scale). We also show the daily increasement of the count of tweets (measured as #TweetsToday/#TweetsYesterday) side by side.
