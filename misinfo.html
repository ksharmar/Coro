Coronavirus on Social Media: Analyzing Twitter conversations


Social media contains bots that are automated or semi-automated. Botometer scores user accounts based on their network activities to estimate the probabilities that the account exhibits characteristics of social bots. We analyze the 500 most retweeted accounts on ongoing coronavirus discussions.
