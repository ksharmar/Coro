Coronavirus on Social Media: Misinformation Analysis


The 'infodemic' on Social Media impacts health and welfare of society, both at a local and global scale. We analyze twitter information cascades to detect and combat misinformation. We expose and maintain a list of false, misleading and clickbait claims detected from information cascades about Coronavirus from March 9 to 17, 2020.
